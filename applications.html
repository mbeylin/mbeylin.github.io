Some of the Use Cases Built on Rubix

Decentralized Capital Markets

A frictionless capital markets system enabled by digital tokens stored on the blockchain, representing existing financial instruments. Records of ownership and automatic exchanges are self executing and tracable

Peer to Peer Payments

A distributed ledger shared by financial institutions, equipped to handle large or small sum transfers. This simplifies settlement and records between financial institutions by leveraging cryptocurrencies

Health Data Management

Store medical records across jurisdictions, creating auditable proof of procedures taking place. Utilizing smart contracts fully ensures data privacy while allowing stakeholders permissioned data accessability

Industry Projects

In collaboration with Estey-Hoover, a New York based strategic communications firm, we are building a blockchain solution for the alternative asset management industry, with the objective of increasing efficiency, enabling transparency, and protecting investor assets.
